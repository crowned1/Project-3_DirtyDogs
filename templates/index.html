Hello Welcome To Dirty Dogs Finder

Have you ever come accross a cute picture of a pooch and wished you knew what breed it was so you can adopt one? If so, you have come to right place!
Dirty Dogs Finder is a site which will assist you in identifying a breed of a dog by simply uploading an image. Our application will analyze your image and identify the breed of dog for you, we will also provide you with information to find your new best friend!

How it works

We use advanced Machine Learning technology and Deep Neural Networks to power our "Dog Gone" software. Once your dog's pic is uploaded our software utilizes Computer Vision and Machine Learning to identify facial key points from the image. These are used to extract SIFT descriptors and color histograms, using SVM with a linear kernel it can predict a dogs breed 90% of the time.
